Uthmani Font Samples

Same Chapter 1 phrase rendered in each candidate font. The KFGQPC Uthman Taha Naskh is the reference face used by the King Fahd Quran Complex and matches what you see in printed Mushafs. Adjust size and try other phrases with the controls below.

How to choose — quick guidance
  • KFGQPC Uthman Taha Naskh — closest to the standard Madinah Mushaf. Best for authenticity. Recommended default.
  • Amiri Quran — open-source, designed specifically for Quran with proper diacritic stacking and pause marks. Excellent free alternative; renders identically on every system.
  • me_quran — Madinah-style, sharp on screen at small sizes.
  • Scheherazade New — very readable Naskh, broad glyph coverage. Less "Mushaf-like" but easier for beginners.
  • Noto Naskh Arabic — modern, universally available, but lacks some Quranic-specific marks.
  • PDMS Saleem — Indo-Pak style (different orthography). Don't pick this unless you want Indo-Pak rasm.

If any card shows the phrase in your system font (looks identical to the others) the CDN font failed to load — refresh, or that face isn't reachable.
